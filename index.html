Lifeng SHEN | 沈礼锋


I am a PhD student in Artificial Intelligence at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), co-advised by Prof. James T. Kwok and Prof. Pan Hui.

Previously, I completed my MPhil at the School of Computer Science and Engineering at the South China University of Technology (SCUT), under the supervision of Prof. Qianli Ma.

My research interests include time series modeling, multiscale analysis, recurrent neural networks/reservoir computing, generative modeling, representation learning.

Lifeng SHEN | 沈礼锋

Doing what you like is freedom. Liking what you do is happiness.

Lifeng Shen is an Associate Professor in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications.

He received his Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2024. His supervisors are Prof. James T. Kwok and Prof. Pan Hui. He previously received his MPhil in CSE from the South China University of Technology, supervised by Prof. Qianli Ma.

His current research interests include: deep learning, time series analysis, reservoir computing, and generative methods.
